Our Commitment to Your Pet

Hi, I’m Yvonne Jensen, the founder and lead walker at Leashed. My mission is simple: to provide a safe, reliable, and fun service that enriches the lives of the pets in my care.

We want to give owners assurances. Every animal is treated like they are our own. I am a Certified Nurse Assistant, First Aid Certified, and a Cardiac Tech. I bring the same level of compassion and care that I offer my patients to your animals.
